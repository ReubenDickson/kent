Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the OML Conference

What's the format of the conference?

The OML Conference features 7 daily sessions (Sunday to Saturday) held live via Zoom at 5:30 PM CST / 6:30 PM EST. Each 90-minute session includes a main presentation or firechat (interactive discussion), Q&A time, and optional networking. Sessions will also be recorded for replay access.

What if I can't attend all sessions live?

No problem! All registered attendees will receive access to session replays. Early Bird tickets include 30-day access, Standard tickets include 90-day access, and VIP tickets include lifetime access to replays.

What's the difference between a firechat and regular session?

Firechats are more interactive, conversational sessions where the speaker engages directly with participants in a dialogue format rather than a traditional lecture. Regular sessions are more presentation-style but still include Q&A opportunities.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you attend the first two sessions and don't find value in the conference, we'll refund your registration fee no questions asked.

What technology do I need to participate?

You'll need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access. We recommend using Zoom for the best interactive experience, but sessions will also be streamed on Facebook for viewing. All replays will be available on YouTube.

How do the VIP one-on-one coaching sessions work?

VIP attendees will receive scheduling instructions after registration to book their 45-minute private coaching session with one of our speakers. These sessions typically occur within 30 days after the conference.
